EDAD-601: College Teaching

Exploring student cognitive development and teaching thinking in higher education.

This space collects materials for my EDAD-601 classroom teaching project: activities, reflections, and interactive tools focused on how students think and how instructors can better support that thinking.

Project overview

A brief description of what lives in this EDAD-601 area of the site.

As part of EDAD-601, I designed and taught a lesson on student cognitive development and obstacles to higher-order thinking. The materials here document that lesson: the interactive website, supporting activities, and follow-up reflections.

You can use these pages as a reference for future teaching, or as examples of how web-based tools can support college teaching.
