Sternberg’s Triarchic Intelligence

Analytic
Analyzing, comparing, evaluating—componential skills.
Creative
Generating ideas, coping with novelty, insight.
Practical
Applying knowledge in real contexts; adapting, shaping, selecting environments.

Kolb’s Experiential Cycle

Concrete experience
Doing/having an experience; direct engagement.
Reflective observation
Watching/reflecting; considering multiple perspectives.
Abstract conceptualization
Forming theories and concepts.
Active experimentation
Testing ideas in practice.
