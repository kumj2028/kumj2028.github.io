Non-digital prototype

Board-game style prototype used to validate the core ideas.

Purpose

Before writing code, the team built a non-digital version of Reflexio to test the reflection mechanic, puzzle feel, and level structure. This allowed rapid iteration on rules without the overhead of implementation.

Rules of play

The rules document describes how players move a token representing Joey on a printed grid, how the reflection line is placed and invoked, and how goals and hazards work in this physical format.

  • Turn structure and actions available each turn.
  • When and how reflections can be triggered.
  • Conditions for success (reaching the exit) and failure.
