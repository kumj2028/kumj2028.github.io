Game manual (player-facing)

Short summary of the manual for Reflexio.

System requirements

  • Windows Vista / XP / 7 or similar.
  • DirectX 9.0c with Shader Model 1.1 support.
  • Approximately 512 MB of RAM.
  • ~100 MB of disk space.

Objective

Guide Joey to the level’s exit door while collecting zippers (keys) and avoiding hazards like spikes and bottomless pits. Use the reflection mechanic to reconfigure the level and reach otherwise impossible locations.

Controls (as described for players)

  • Arrow keys: move and jump Joey.
  • W/A/S/D/X: position the reflection axis.
  • R: reflect the world about the current axis.
  • Other keys: as described in the full manual / in-game help.

Game elements

  • Joey: the player-controlled baby koala.
  • Blocks: basic geometry that can often be reflected to form walls or floors.
  • Non-reflectable blocks: special tiles that do not move when the world is reflected, anchoring parts of the puzzle.
  • Buddy blocks & switches: moveable blocks and switches that open doors when activated.
  • Zippers: collectibles required to open the exit door.
  • Spikes and pits: instant-death hazards to avoid.
